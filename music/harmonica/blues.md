
### Blues Scale

Blues Scale is a set of notes going from 2 hole draw to 6 hole blow, from root to octave.

Scale Degrees
> Root, Blue 3rd, Fourth, Flat Fifth, Fifth, Flat Seven, Oactave (and reverse)
